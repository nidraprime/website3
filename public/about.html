Meet the Founder
Amit Kumar Jain
Founder & Managing Director · 26 Years Experience

Amit Kumar Jain began his journey in the foam industry in 1998 with a simple but powerful belief: that every Indian deserves sleep that heals. Starting with a small retail unit in Delhi NCR, he spent years studying international foam technology while adapting it to India's climatic conditions, body types, and sleeping habits.

His collaboration with orthopedic specialists at Jaipur's SMS Medical College led to the development of NidraPrime's signature "Zoned Support System" — a patented approach to foam layering that provides firm lumbar support while allowing shoulder and hip sinkage, achieving true spinal neutrality.

"I have seen too many patients whose back problems were directly linked to poor sleep surfaces," says Amit. "My life's work is to change that — one mattress at a time."

Design Philosophy

The Orthopedic Advantage

Our foam layers aren't randomly arranged — they follow biomechanical principles to support your body the way it was designed to rest.

Spinal Alignment

A neutral spine during sleep reduces disc pressure by up to 70% compared to misaligned sleeping positions. Our zoned foam layers maintain this alignment whether you sleep on your back, side, or stomach.

Pressure Point Relief

Traditional mattresses create pressure hotspots at shoulders, hips, and heels. NidraPrime's memory foam layer conforms to these areas, distributing weight evenly and improving circulation during sleep.

Motion Isolation

Our premium layers absorb movement transfer between sleep partners. Tests show less than 15% motion transfer — meaning your partner's movements won't disrupt your deep sleep cycles.

Temperature Regulation

India's tropical climate demands breathable sleep surfaces. Our open-cell foam technology and gel-infused layers actively draw heat away from the body, keeping you 2–4°C cooler through the night.
