Warranty Terms
  • Warranty is limited to manufacturing defects, sagging, and crumbling under proper use of the product.

This Mattress Warranty Does Not Cover
  • There is no warranty for the fabric used as covering on the mattress.
  • Warranty shall be void if the mattress is folded, ironed, bent, rolled improperly, or misused.
  • Physical damage caused by improper bed support, moisture, insects, bed bugs, stains, burns, or mishandling.
  • With normal use, foam may develop softness and change in colour — these shall not be considered manufacturing defects.
  • Change in comfort preference or normal softening over time shall not be treated as a manufacturing defect.

NidraPrime Pro Rata Policy

How Pro-Rata Value Is Calculated

Initial warranty period: free replacement/guarantee. Remaining warranty period: partial charges applicable based on usage period.

Monthly Depreciation  =  Mattress Price  ÷  (Warranty Years × 12)
Worked Example
Mattress price: ₹6,000, Warranty: 5 years (60 months)
Monthly depreciation = ₹6,000 ÷ 60 = ₹100 per month
Months 1–12 (initial warranty period): ₹0 charged — free replacement/guarantee
From month 13 onward (remaining warranty period): ₹100 is deducted from covered value every month
Example: a claim in month 20 has depreciated by (20 − 12) × ₹100 = ₹800 — that ₹800 is the customer's share, and NidraPrime covers the remaining value

You can see your own mattress's live pro-rata value any time from My Account → Warranty Status.

Mattress fabric must not be torn, cut, stained, or strained. In such cases, fabric replacement charges will be applicable separately.
